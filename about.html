How it works

Behind the Choices

A simple, straightforward look at how this test finds your historical match without any confusing formulas or tech talk.

You'll answer a handful of everyday scenarios — nothing tricky, just how you'd naturally react. Your choices build a picture of your habits and instincts, and the test compares that picture against ten historical figures to find your closest match. If two matches end up close, one final question settles it.
