How it works

Behind the Choices

A simple, plain explanation of this test with no technical jargon or complicated details.

1. You answer ten questions

Each question is about a normal, daily situation. How you organize your day, buy things, deal with offenses, or react to set backs. There are no trick questions. Just choose the one that feels most natural to you.

2. Your habits are weighed

Every answer carries different points. Some choices point to one specific person from history, while others share points across a few different profiles depending on how they lived their lives.

3. Find your historical match

We show you who your choices align with out of ten distinct profiles. You will see how you match their real strengths, personal vulnerabilities, and a tiny story from their actual lives.

What we collect

Your personal test answers stay entirely inside your browser window. They are never sent to a database, stored, or linked to you.

The only things we track are basic layout specs: your screen resolution, browser style, and the visited page path. This purely helps us keep the elements looking clean and aligned. No names, no emails, and absolutely zero identifiers.
