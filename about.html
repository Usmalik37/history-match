How it works

Behind the Choices

A simple, straightforward look at how this test finds your historical match without any confusing formulas or tech talk.

1. You answer everyday scenarios

Each question looks at a normal, real-life situation—like how you map out tasks, handle a busy day, deal with mistakes, or clear unexpected hurdles. No trick choices, just pick what you would actually do.

2. Your patterns take shape

As you move through the test, your answers connect you to different leaders and thinkers. Your choices might point cleanly to one specific person, or show a unique mix of traits shared across a few different minds.

3. Finding your final match

If your results end up in a close tie between two profiles at the very end, the test will give you one final question to break it. You'll get your exact historical match along with a breakdown of how you both get things done.

Your Privacy

Your answers and choices stay entirely on your own device. Everything is processed safely right inside your web browser, which means your choices are never sent to a database, stored online, or tracked across the web.

We only look at basic, anonymous usage details — like how many people visit and which pages get used — to understand how the test is doing. We don't use tracking cookies, require log-ins, or collect personal information.
